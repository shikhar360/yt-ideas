# The Ultimate Developer Workflow

## Pain Points
- Manual repetitive tasks
- Context switching
- Lack of documentation

## Benefit
Save 10 hours a week with automation

## Steps
- **The Blueprint**: Map out your most frequent manual steps.
- **The Script**: Automate the mapping using simple Node.js scripts.
- **The Feedback Loop**: Continuously refine based on actual usage.

## The Trap to Avoid
Don't try to automate everything at once - start small and iterate.
