# Source: Productivity Frameworks for Software Engineers

## Summary
A compilation of psychological and organizational frameworks (Deep Work, Flow State, Maker's Schedule) specifically adapted for software engineering.

## Key Findings
- **90-Minute Focus**: The minimum block for complex mental model loading.
- **Attention Residue**: The cognitive cost of switching tasks or checking Slack, which lingers and reduces IQ.
- **Shutdown Ritual**: A formal end to the day to capture "open loops" and protect rest periods.
- **Immediate Feedback**: The critical role of TDD and Hot Reload in maintaining flow.
- **Maker vs. Manager**: Why a single mid-afternoon meeting can destroy a developer's entire day.
